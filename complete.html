What We Deliver

Our Core Capabilities

Step 01

Design & CAD

We translate your sketches or references into ultra-precise 3D CAD files ready for prototyping, with full revision support before anything is manufactured.

Our expert design team combines artistic vision with technical precision to create detailed CAD models that capture every nuance of your vision. We understand that perfection lies in the details, and every millimeter matters in luxury jewelry. Multiple revision rounds are included until you're completely satisfied with the design.

Key Features

  • 3D CAD Modeling
  • Prototype Samples
  • Design Consultation
  • Wax Approval
Step 02

Casting Run

Precious metals are cast using controlled lost-wax methods with strict alloy accuracy, ensuring consistent weight and purity across every batch.

Using time-honored lost-wax casting techniques combined with modern precision equipment, we ensure every piece meets exacting standards. Our expert founders control every variable in the casting process to achieve perfect results. Each batch undergoes rigorous quality checks for weight, dimensions, and material purity before moving to the next stage.

Key Features

  • Silver 925 & Gold
  • Lost-wax Method
  • Alloy Control
  • Post-cast QC
Step 03

Elite Polishing

Every surface is refined by hand and machine finishing to achieve flawless mirror shine, matte texture, or brushed detailing — even on complex mesh work.

Our master polishers combine traditional hand techniques with advanced machinery to achieve the perfect finish you envision. From high-gloss mirror finishes that catch light beautifully to subtle matte textures that convey sophistication, we deliver consistent luxury results. We handle even the most intricate geometric patterns and complex mesh structures with precision.

Key Features

  • Mirror Polish
  • Matte & Brushed
  • Hand Finishing
  • Complex Mesh
Step 04

Stone Setting

Our setters specialize in secure prong, bezel and pavé techniques for diamonds, CZ, and natural gemstones, matched to your design's exact tolerances.

Each stone is set with precision and care by our expert setters who have years of experience securing stones in any configuration. We protect your precious materials from damage while ensuring absolute security. Whether you're working with conflict-free diamonds, cubic zirconia for samples, or precious gemstones, our team handles every stone with the utmost care and expertise.

Key Features

  • Prong & Bezel
  • Pavé Setting
  • CZ & Diamonds
  • Gemstones
Step 05

Premium Plating

Durable plating layers protect and enhance color — from warm 18k gold and rose gold to bright rhodium and deep black rhodium finishes.

Our advanced plating process ensures durability and beauty that lasts. We offer a full range of finishes to complement any design aesthetic, from classic yellow and white gold tones to trendy rose gold and sophisticated black rhodium. Each plating layer is applied with consistent thickness and perfect adhesion for long-lasting wear and protection.

Key Features

  • 18k Gold Plating
  • Rose Gold Plating
  • Rhodium Layer
  • Black Rhodium
Step 06

Quality Control

Before shipping, every piece passes XRF composition testing, sizing checks, and micro inspection, then leaves in secure, tamper-proof packaging.

Nothing leaves our facility without meeting our exacting standards. Each piece undergoes multiple inspection points to ensure it matches your specifications perfectly. We use advanced XRF (X-ray fluorescence) technology to verify metal composition and purity. Our meticulous attention to detail in quality control guarantees that your customers receive jewelry that exceeds expectations every single time.

Key Features

  • XRF Composition
  • Sizing Accuracy
  • Micro Inspection
  • Secure Packing
