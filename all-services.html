Design & Engineering

Design & CAD

From Concept Sketch to Manufacturable Precision

Every collection begins on the design floor. Our in-house CAD engineers translate raw sketches, moodboards or reference jewelry into fully dimensioned, production-ready 3D files — balancing artistic intent with the structural realities of casting, stone setting and daily wear.

  • 3D CAD Modeling

    Every piece is rebuilt in native CAD software with exact wall thickness, prong geometry and stone seating calculated to manufacturing tolerance, so what you approve on screen is exactly what gets cast.

  • Prototype Samples

    Before a full production run begins, a physical 3D-printed or wax prototype is produced so you can inspect scale, proportion and comfort in hand.

  • Design Consultation

    A dedicated designer works directly with your brand team through revision rounds, translating feedback into technical changes without losing the original vision.

  • Wax Approval

    A final wax or resin master model is signed off by your team before casting authorization, giving you a last checkpoint to catch any adjustment.

Production

Casting Run

Precision Lost-Wax Casting in Gold & Silver 925

Our casting floor runs certified lost-wax casting lines calibrated for both silver 925 and gold alloys, with every batch tracked from wax tree to finished shell for full material traceability.

  • Silver 925 & Gold

    Casting lines are configured separately for silver 925 and multiple gold karats to avoid cross-contamination and preserve alloy purity.

  • Lost-Wax Method

    Traditional lost-wax investment casting is used to capture fine detail, intricate filigree and complex undercuts with minimal porosity.

  • Alloy Control

    Every melt is measured and logged against target alloy composition, keeping color, hardness and karat consistent across production batches.

  • Postcast QC

    Each cast tree is inspected straight out of the flask for porosity, incomplete fills or shrinkage before moving to the finishing stage.

Finishing

Elite Polishing

Hand-Finished Surfaces, Mirror to Matte

Polishing is where a casting becomes jewelry. Our finishing team hand-works every surface transition, edge and recess to deliver the exact finish specified — from a high mirror shine to a brushed, contemporary matte.

  • Mirror Polish

    Multi-stage buffing brings surfaces to a high-gloss, distortion-free mirror finish suitable for statement and bridal pieces.

  • Matte & Brushed

    Controlled abrasive finishing produces an even, directional matte or brushed texture for a modern, tactile look.

  • Hand Finishing

    Detail areas that machines can't safely reach — tight filigree, inner bands, prong bases — are finished entirely by hand.

  • Complex Mesh

    Chainmail, mesh and multi-link constructions are polished piece by piece to keep flexibility intact while achieving a uniform shine.

Stone Work

Stone Setting

Secure, Symmetrical Setting for Every Stone Type

Our setters specialize in high-volume, consistent stone setting across diamonds, cubic zirconia and colored gemstones, holding tight symmetry and security across every piece in a run.

  • Prong & Bezel

    Classic prong and bezel settings are shaped and closed by hand to hold each stone securely while keeping the metal frame clean and minimal.

  • Pavé Setting

    Small stones are set edge-to-edge in tight, even rows for a continuous sparkle across bands, halos and clusters.

  • CZ & Diamonds

    Setting protocols are adjusted for stone hardness and cut, covering both cubic zirconia commercial lines and fine diamond work.

  • Gemstones

    Colored gemstones are set with attention to heat sensitivity and durability, protecting softer stones through the setting process.

Surface Treatment

Premium Plating

Durable Gold, Rose Gold & Rhodium Layers

Plating is the final layer of color and protection. Our electroplating lines apply consistent, durable coatings measured in microns, so every piece in a collection matches in tone and wear resistance.

  • 18k Gold Plating

    A rich, consistent 18k gold layer is applied over base metal or silver for a premium finish at accessible production cost.

  • Rose Gold Plating

    Warm rose-gold tones are achieved through calibrated alloy plating baths, matched precisely across an entire production batch.

  • Rhodium Layer

    A rhodium topcoat adds a bright white shine and a protective barrier that slows tarnish and everyday surface wear.

  • Black Rhodium

    Black rhodium plating delivers a deep, matte-to-gloss dark finish for contemporary and statement pieces.

Quality Assurance

Quality Control

Every Piece Verified Before It Ships

Before any order leaves our facility, it passes through a dedicated QC checkpoint covering material composition, sizing accuracy, surface inspection and secure packing — giving your brand a consistent, verified product every time.

  • XRF Composition

    XRF spectrometry testing confirms actual metal composition against the specified alloy or karat before pieces are approved.

  • Sizing Accurate

    Ring sizes, chain lengths and stone dimensions are measured against original spec sheets to catch drift before shipment.

  • Micro Inspection

    Pieces are examined under magnification for setting security, surface defects and finish consistency.

  • Secure Packing

    Approved pieces are individually packed with protective materials suited for international freight and retail handoff.
