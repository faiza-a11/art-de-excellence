The Atelier Workflow

Six Stages of Fabrication

01. Concept & Technical CAD

Translating design sketches into parametric 3D CAD files calibrated for precise metal shrinkage and exact gemstone carat dimensions.

02. 3D Resin Prototyping

Printing high-resolution 10-micron wax resin models for physical inspection, weight testing, and master rubber mold preparation.

03. Vacuum Induction Casting

Liquefying certified 18K Gold, Platinum, or 925 Silver into oxygen-free vacuum induction chambers to eliminate porosity.

04. Micro-Pave Setting

Hand-setting natural diamonds or precious gemstones under 40x zoom Leica stereo microscopes for optimal light refraction.

Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions regarding our OEM/ODM manufacturing processes, order volumes, alloys, and timelines.

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) for custom production?

We work flexibly with luxury partners. For custom CAD production, our standard MOQ starts at 10 pieces per design, while full collection ODM orders are tailored according to specific precious metal weights and gemstone requirements.

How long does a full production cycle take from design to delivery?

Initial 3D CAD modeling and rendering are completed within 3 to 5 business days. Once approved, wax printing, casting, setting, and hand-polishing take approximately 3 to 4 weeks depending on order volume and design complexity.

How do you protect intellectual property and custom designs?

All client partnerships are protected under legally binding non-disclosure agreements (NDAs). Your 3D CAD files and custom molds are stored securely and never reused, displayed, or shared without prior written consent.

Do you provide certified metal hallmarks and gem testing?

Yes. All precious metal pieces are XRF-analyzed and stamped with certified purity hallmarks (e.g., 750 for 18K, 950 for Platinum). Independent laboratory certificates (GIA, IGI) can be supplied for center diamonds and precious gems.

Can you accommodate client-supplied gemstones or diamonds?

Absolutely. We accept customer-provided gems via insured courier. Our gemologists inspect each stone upon arrival, record precise millimeter dimensions and laser inscriptions, and calibrate CAD seats for zero setting tension.

What shipping carriers and insurance protocols are utilized?

All international orders are dispatched via armored logistics (FedEx International Priority with Malca-Amit or Brink's insurance coverage). Every shipment is fully insured from atelier exit to final door-to-door delivery signature.
